Teaching Philosophy

I believe in fostering a deep understanding of computer science and AI through active learning and hands-on experiences. During my time as a tutor at the University of Otago, I focused on guiding students through challenging programming and computer science concepts, adapting my approach to meet diverse learning styles. I emphasize a student-centered approach, providing one-on-one support and encouraging collaboration to ensure students grasp fundamental skills in programming, data communications, and problem-solving. My goal is to create an inclusive and engaging learning environment where students not only build technical proficiency but also develop the critical thinking needed to apply these skills in practical, real-world scenarios.
